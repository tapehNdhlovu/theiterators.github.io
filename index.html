About Us

Our Mision

To impact the challenged women and children in the society through practical demonstration of love by building sustainable projects that can support them until they are skilled enough to become independent.

Our Vision

To change lives and restore hope to the hurting, abused women and abandoned children by empowering them, building up women and children of honor and dignity that will impact the world and be sought after in the society.

Our Work

What we do ?

For Women

  • Development of local small scale businesses within communities for the use of women and children to enhance community development and foster unity in rural communities
  • Implement the approved program on women’s development
  • Monitor and evaluate programs on women’s development
  • Impart the necessary skill training to women
  • Carry out functions related to women’s empowerment.
  • Carry out functions related to national or international seminars, symposia and conferences on women
  • Collect and publish information on women’s development.

For Children

  • Provision of facilities for rehabilitating and resettling children with their mothers
  • Advocating for the protection of children’s rights
  • Providing educational/ vocational assistance for children and youth
  • Ensuring economic advancement for the youth.
